What are we doing for the welfare of the society????

We are distributing the ration to the people who have lost their bread earners of the family in this tough times. So that they can survive after big loses of their family.
We have started the free education for the students of school and college who have lost the bread earners of their family.
We are trying to provide the employment opportunities to all the capable members of the family whether they are illiterate or literate, they will get the desired job to earn the living.

What are our future goals?

After helping all the families who are highly affected in terms of financial conditions. We will be focusing on all the people who have lost their jobs whether they are literate or illiterate, we will be helping them with the ration, free education of their children and employment opportunities for them.
